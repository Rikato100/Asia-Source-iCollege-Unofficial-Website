PRIVACY NOTICE

At Asia Source iCollege (ASiC), we deeply respect and honor your rights as a data subject under the Data Privacy Act (DPA). We are dedicated to safeguarding the personal information you share with us, following the guidelines set by the DPA and its Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR). To ensure the security of your personal data, ASiC employs reasonable and suitable security measures to keep your information confidential, intact, and available. For more information about our Privacy Policy, you can request our College Data Privacy Policy from our staff or visit the Data Protection Officer (DPO) office.

In the usual course of our operations, members of our departments responsible for collecting personal information may gather your data. This happens when you decide to enroll or join the ASiC community, either as a student or an employee. We may collect and store details like your name, address, contact information, gender, marital status, religious beliefs, health information, previous jobs, educational background, and other personal details. For those who are part of our community, we also keep records of your academic performance, grades, disciplinary actions, and other details related to your college experience.

By choosing to join our College, applying for a job, or providing us with your personal details, you are giving your consent to ASiC's Data Privacy Policy. You allow Asia Source iCollege to collect, store, and process your personal data within the Philippines, either manually or electronically. This will be done for the duration mentioned in ASiC's Data Privacy Manual or its Department IRR, or as permitted by the relevant laws and regulations. Your personal information will be used for legitimate business purposes, as required by government regulatory bodies, and to provide you with quality services. Unless you decide to withdraw your consent, this agreement will remain valid.
